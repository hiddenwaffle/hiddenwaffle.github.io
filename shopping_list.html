🥬 Produce Weekly
Carrots — 1 lb bag
Lasts 2+ weeks. Use on chicken day.
Bell peppers — 2–3
Any color. Lasts ~1 week. Works with all proteins.
Zucchini — 2 medium
Use within 4–5 days. Works with all proteins.
Green beans — ½ lb
Lasts ~1 week. Chicken or beef day.
Broccoli — 1 head or bag
Use within 4–5 days. Chicken or beef day. Moderate nickel—don't overdo.
Cauliflower — 1 head
Lasts ~1 week. Chicken day. Moderate nickel—don't overdo.
Cherry tomatoes — 1 pint
Lasts ~1 week. Add last 10–15 min (or at start for salmon).
Lemon — 1
For zest. Mediterranean seasoning.
Fresh ginger — small knob
Asian-inspired. Freezes well—grate from frozen.
Pick 3–4 vegetables per week based on which proteins you're cooking
🥩 Meat & Seafood Weekly
Chicken thighs — 1.5 lbs
Bone-in, skin-on. Freeze if not cooking within 2 days.
Ground beef — 1 lb
80-85% lean. Freeze if not cooking within 2 days.
Salmon — 1 lb
Fillet. Most perishable—cook first or freeze.
Stock Up Once
🧊 Condiments/Refrigerated Stock Up

Italian seasoning. Drizzle after cooking. Keeps indefinitely.

Long grain. Keeps indefinitely in pantry.
